Our estimation method
Whether you’re ready to sell or just plain curious, our website provides a first estimation of your property.
Our method is based on a machine learning algorithm.
We use a Gradient Boosting Regressor trained on a large dataset of houses in the US and we account for many elements of a property that we will ask you about when you enter into our estimation simulator.
If you wish to sell and once you have done your online estimation, you can contact us through our Contact page and we will get back to you.

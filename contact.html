About us
Welcome to Triple H, your number one source for Real Estate estimation.
We're dedicated to giving you the very best of House forecasting with a focus on accuracy,
reliability and uniqueness.
Founded in 1998, Triple H has come a long way from its beginnings in Palaiseau, France.
Three students from ENSAE decided it was time to use data for people to be able to get a first estimate
of their properties before contacting real estate agents.
This first estimate is solely based on numbers and facts so people can grasp the worth of their properties.
We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the challenging wing of
the Real Estate industry.
Since 2014, we also provide our knowledge of real estate to help you sell.
If you wish to sell, please use the form below so we can get in touch.
We hope you enjoy our product as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any
questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
